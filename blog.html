Rebaze joins the OSGi Alliance

As of today, Rebaze is proud to become a member at the OSGi Alliance.

Since its inception, Rebaze is committed to helping software development teams making better software. OSGi as a technology is the only proven runtime level module system for the Java Platform.

Migrating to OSGi is not an easy task. Its usually a painful task. But it is worth the effort as it tells you exactly how good or bad your architecture really is. On the other hand, chosing OSGi as a technology to start with is also full of possible pitfalls.  There are many way of how to actually use OSGi. Go OSGi-first or embedd the OSGi as a plugin kernel into another JEE Container ? How to actually develop and test bundles ?

As a Contributing Associate Member, Rebaze is able to participate in specification discussions helping making OSGi easier to adopt and more useful to the enterprise software project.

Rebaze launch FAQ

Good Morning again,

Thank you all for your great feedback and suggestions after our initial launch less than 2 weeks ago. We also got a ton of questions some of which i'd like to answer in this blog now.

Where does the name Rebaze come from ?

Rebaze is an artificial spelling of the term rebase used in the git source control system. We are fitting the term rebase onto rebasing software development teams. Also, the domain rebaze.com was available at that point in time ;) 

Making Software Run is about what we believe in: software. Actually not dead (as in code) software but building software artifacts and running this. Filling the gap between dead code and vivid platforms is essential to Rebaze.

Birthdate ? How many rebazers ? You have offices ?

The Domain was registered on January, 14th 2012.

We have three founders and a growing number of distributed experts assigned on demand currently.

Offices are where you are. Rebaze is a distributed company with customers, partners and employees around the globe. As a client you can be sure we are working in the most productive at places we know and like.

What's alien technology ?

We are driven by the passion for making software development better. This is why Rebaze exists. This is our product. Or put it annother way: make less projects fail because of poorly understood tooling or fear to adopt alien technology. We fix this (here it comes) with mix of unique service offerings, open source enabled expertize and inhouse technology.

In some regard we are believers feeling the need for more technical focus specially in the enterrpise world again after years of agile movement. Its not done yet. Yet we feel that teams are not catching up fast enough with technologies that drives (and are driven by) the innovaters in the market: NoSQL (Neo4j anyone ?), BigData and alternative JVM languages like Scala. 

Don't get us started on the state of build- and configuration management solution - something that open source commities are lightyears ahead (Git, Gerrit, declarative builds, automated & rock solid releases)

 

Will there be a Rebaze Pass for X ?

On July 4th we launched two passes, for Maven and OSGi. We are working on additional passes to be launched in near future. Keep in mind that we are only offering Passes to technologies that we think can make a key difference in a software development project. We do believe in rocket science technology combined with expert assistance in order to create sustainable and innovative platforms and products.

In that regard, Rebaze Pass can even be some kind of a technology radar for you.
