    Introducing Rebaze Technology

  • Rebaze is a privately held company in Germany (imprint).

    We founded the Rebaze GmbH on April, 1st, 2012 after about 10 years of consulting and open source work in the enterprise sector. Rebaze has a solid foundation of experience in Java & OSGi Technology.

    The project history of its founders include clients from Automotive, Banking, Government, IT and Airline/Travel industries as well as pharmaceutics.
    The founders share a passion for open source software development and do participate actively at OPS4J.org, Codehaus.org and Apache Software Foundation (ASF) for a long time.
